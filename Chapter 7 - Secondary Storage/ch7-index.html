Secondary Storage

Why should you read this page?

If you don't have the appropriate secondary storage, your computer will slow down, your digital camera won't take pictures, and your phone won't be able to run apps. In the future, that will likely not be a problem as we will see storage types can hold the entire UIDAI (Unique Identification Authority of India) data on a single disk as small as a coin and store information in holograms or even organic molecules.
This page covers the things you need know to be prepared for this ever-changing digital world, including:

  1. Getting the appropriate hard drive on your computer to meet all you needs.
  2. Sharing digital information on Blu-ray, CD, or DVD.
  3. Making your portable electronics faster and use less power.
  4. Storing your information safely and securely on the Internet.
